goals

Activities is an ICT Systems Integrator and Solution Provider, specialized in Security Systems and Software solutions. Established in 1999, we have accumulated a wealth of experience that enables us to offer a wide range of services,

  • 1- Empowering youth economically and enhancing their community, political and civic participation, freedom of expression and advocacy for their causes.
  • 2- Work to alleviate poverty and support livelihoods.
  • 3- Contribute to the promotion of the principles of democracy, human rights, good governance, integrity and combating corruption in order to consolidate the concepts of civil society in accordance with international humanitarian law.
  • 4- Work on the social empowerment of women and the protection of their rights.
  • 5- Develop the capabilities of the various segments of society through educational, sports, health, psychological, social, relief and early childhood programs to contribute to achieving sustainable development.
  • 6- Building the capacity of the association, its employees and volunteers.
